About Me

To briefly describe myself, I was born and raised in Mexico. I came to the United States as an Au Pair. As an Au Pair I took care of kids between 6 and 10 years old. A couple years before I came to the U.S. as an Au Pair, I had the opportunity to support and participate in a couple of groups of the Sunday school for children at church. I served a year with the kids between 7 to 10 years old, and another year with the 4 to 6 years old little ones. That experience and my love for children, opened me the door to get into the Au Pair program. My current job since November 2018 is as a Spanish teacher assistant in a preschool, so this schedule allows me to get a second job during the evenings and weekends. I like being active with kids and teach them my native language!.
